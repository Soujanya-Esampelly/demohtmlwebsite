SWATCH BHARATH

As a part of village development plans we focussed on each element in each week.



DAY1

In this week we focussed on the drainage system.we observed how the drainage systems are there.



Day2

Later we asked some of the villagers about the drainage systems.Then they said that the drainage system is not good.So much garbage gets stuck in the drainage.When it rains, the drainage gets filled.


Day3

Some people also came to know that they were ruining the drainage of their village


DAY4

Then we gave awareness on how to improve the drainage systems.



DAY5

We have said that if the drainage systems are not good,they will affect on them.And as we said,having such drainage can cause many health problems for you and your children.Also,mosquitoes come from it,and there is a chance that they will be attacked by many viruses



DAY6
